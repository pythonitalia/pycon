Travel grant

All grants involving reimbursements will be reimbursed based on approved receipts (plane/bus/train ticket) sent to us before the conference. The reimbursement will be made by a bank transfer or with PayPal after the conference.

Please note:

  • Not approved invoices will not be reimbursed.
  • Reimbursements before the conference are not possible.
  • The financial aid grants will only be made available to registered attendees actually attending the conference.
  • Transfers to other people are not possible.
  • As part of our efforts to reduce the carbon footprint of the conference we expect our attendees to use trains rather than airplanes when possible. Thus, we will not refund air travel costs when other means of transportation are easily available. This will be considered on a case by case basis. Please consider this when calculating the estimated costs.
